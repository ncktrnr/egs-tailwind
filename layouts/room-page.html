‘Terraza’ immerses you in the slow pace of the España Verde. Awake to birdsong and the gentle toll of cowbells; inhale the crisp mountain air, then watch the mist rise over the Sierra del Sueve and the Picos de Europa mountain ranges from a private terrace inspired by traditional Asturian balconies. Decorated in muted tones with a ceiling rising to four metres – and with graceful, sculptural furniture by modernist designers Eileen Gray and Eero Saarinen – this is a space in which to reset and find stillness.

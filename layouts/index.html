A boutique hotel in a traditional Asturian country house where simple, contemporary design meets traditional Spanish charm. Surrounded by views of the Sierra del Sueve and Picos de Europa, El gran sueño invites you to embrace a slower pace of life and immerse yourself in the landscape, culture and gastronomy of the España Verde.

Rooms

Our five hotel rooms have a distinctive character and their own unique outlook: choose from misty mountain vistas, Juliet balconies overlooking a tranquil village square or a private terrace looking out over a beautifully-tended garden. Ideal for guests seeking a personal experience with local insights, organic breakfasts and home-cooked evening meals by reservation.
